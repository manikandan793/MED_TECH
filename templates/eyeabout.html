Details of Diseases

Cataract

A clouding of the lens in the eye that affects vision. Cataracts are very common in older people and can lead to blindness if untreated.

Glaucoma

A group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often due to high eye pressure. Glaucoma can lead to blindness if not treated.

Diabetic Retinopathy

A diabetes complication that affects the eyes, caused by damage to the blood vessels of the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye (retina).

Normal Eye

Indicates a healthy eye with no signs of disease or abnormality.
